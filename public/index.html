FAQ
Got Questions?
Do I need a credit card to sign up?
▼
No credit card required for the free plan. You get 2,500 credits every month free, forever. Upgrade only when you need more.
What is a credit?
▼
Credits are consumed when you generate content. Roughly 1 credit = 1 word of output. A typical blog post uses 800–1,200 credits. The AI Chat uses credits too.
Which AI model powers the platform?
▼
M-SM AI uses Groq's ultra-fast LLaMA models for the AI Chat — delivering responses in under a second. Content generation uses Anthropic Claude or OpenAI depending on the task.
Can I use M-SM AI for my clients?
▼
Absolutely. The Agency plan is designed for this. You can manage multiple brand voices, create client workspaces, and export white-labeled content. Many agencies resell M-SM AI as their own service.
Can I cancel anytime?
▼
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period with no questions asked.
